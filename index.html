Qualifications

I have been playing instruments since I was 5 years old, and I have been in a band since I was 10 years old. I am currently in a band called AFTM that plays venues throughout Georgia such as Smith’s Olde Bar, Georgia Theatre Rooftop (coming in April), NoWhere Bar, and other local Athens’ bars. Our band just released a single on all streaming platforms and download services, which I mixed and mastered. We are in the process of recording an EP at 1093 Boulevard this spring.

Our band plays southern rock/jam style music. I also produce electronic music on the side, and I am currently in the process of developing a name/brand for myself with future releases impending.
